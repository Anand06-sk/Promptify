Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us.

This Privacy Policy explains what information Prompixs collects, how we use it, and how we protect it. We believe in being transparent and respectful with the data that helps power your experience on the platform.

Effective Date: To be updated upon official launch
Last Updated: To be updated upon official launch

Introduction

Prompixs is an AI prompt discovery platform where users can discover prompts, search by category or tags, view preview images, bookmark favorites, explore trending and latest collections, open prompts in AI platforms such as ChatGPT and Gemini, and create AI-generated images using carefully crafted prompts together with their own photos.

This Privacy Policy describes the information we collect when you use Prompixs and how that information is used, stored, and protected. By using Prompixs, you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

When you create an account on Prompixs, we collect the information needed to manage your account and personalize your experience. This includes your username and email address.

If you sign in with Google, Prompixs may receive your Google account name, email address, and profile picture from Google to help create and maintain your account.

After signing in, you may optionally provide additional profile details such as your date of birth, gender, and bio. These fields are completely optional and can be edited or removed at any time.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide account access, support your profile, save your bookmarks, and improve the overall experience on Prompixs. We also use certain platform activity data such as prompt likes and prompt views to display popularity and improve how prompts are presented to users.

We do not currently store your search history, browsing history, or recently viewed prompts.

Optional Profile Information

The additional profile details you choose to provide are used only to make your experience on Prompixs more complete and personal. You can update or remove this information whenever you want from your account settings.

Google Sign-In Information

When you sign in with Google, Prompixs uses the account information provided by Google to create and manage your account. Prompixs may use your Google profile picture automatically as your profile picture on the platform. At this time, users cannot upload a custom profile picture directly to Prompixs.

Bookmarks and Platform Statistics

Prompixs stores your bookmarks and account preferences so that you can return to prompts you have saved. We also track prompt likes and prompt views to help show which prompts are popular and to improve the platform experience.

Prompixs does not currently collect search history or browsing history for profiling or long-term tracking purposes.

Cookies and Local Storage

Prompixs may use browser storage features such as local storage to support basic account and interface functionality. This may include remembering preferences or helping the site function properly while you are using it. We do not use these features for unrelated tracking or advertising purposes.

Third-Party Services

Prompixs uses Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, and Firebase Hosting to manage account access, store profile and platform data, and deliver the website securely. Prompixs also works with Cloudinary for hosting prompt preview images, but this is managed by Prompixs administrators. Users do not upload images directly to the platform.

Prompixs also provides quick access to external AI platforms such as ChatGPT and Google Gemini. Once you leave Prompixs and use those services, your activity will be governed by their own privacy practices and terms of use. Prompixs is not responsible for the privacy practices of those third-party services.

Firebase, Google Sign-In, and Cloudinary each have their own Privacy Policies and Terms of Service, and users are encouraged to review those policies when using those services.

Data Security

Prompixs takes reasonable steps to protect user information using the security features provided by Firebase services. We use secure authentication and protected storage practices to reduce the risk of unauthorized access. However, no online platform can guarantee absolute security, and users should also take care to protect their own account credentials.

Although we use strong security measures through Firebase and secure account access, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage can ever be completely secure. We continue to review our practices and improve them as the platform grows.

Account Deletion

If you delete your account, Prompixs will remove your personal account information, your Firebase Authentication account, your profile data, and your bookmarks. This helps ensure that your account-related information is no longer active on the platform.

Aggregated platform statistics such as likes, views, popularity metrics, and analytics may remain because they are not personally identifiable and help maintain the integrity of the platform. We do not remove prompt data, prompt likes, prompt views, prompt statistics, or admin-created content that is part of the platform itself.

Children's Privacy

Prompixs is intended for a general audience and is designed to be used by people who are able to manage an online account responsibly. If a user is under the age required by the laws of their country to manage an online account, they should use Prompixs with the permission or supervision of a parent or legal guardian.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

Prompixs may update this Privacy Policy as the platform evolves and new features are introduced. When significant changes are made, we will notify users through a notice on the website. Please review this page regularly so you are aware of any updates.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please contact us at prompix@gmail.com.
